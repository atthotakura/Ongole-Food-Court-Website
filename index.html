About OFC

Ongole's Favourite Food Hangout

OFC – Ongole Food Court is the neighbourhood go-to for quick bites and comfort food that never disappoints. Whether you're grabbing a meal with family, catching up with college friends, or satisfying a late-night craving, OFC has you covered.

From flame-grilled burgers and crispy chicken to stone-baked pizzas, refreshing mocktails, and indulgent desserts — every visit is a new treat. Come in, sit back, and let the food do the talking.
